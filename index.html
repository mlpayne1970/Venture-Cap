📚 How to Play Venture

1 Place a Tile

Each turn, select a tile from your rack and place it on the board. Tiles are labeled with coordinates (e.g., "3A" means column 3, row A).

2A
3A
4A
2 Found Companies

When you place a tile adjacent to another tile (not part of a company), you can found a new company. Choose from 7 companies, each with different price tiers.

💰 Budget: Scribble, Clickr 💎 Mid-Tier: Velocity, Quantum, Cybr 👑 Luxury: OmniCorp, Apex

💡 Founding a company gives you 1 free share!

3 Buy Shares

After placing your tile, you can buy up to 3 shares per turn from any active companies. Share prices increase as companies grow larger.

2 tiles = $200 → 6 tiles = $600 → 11+ tiles = $800+
4 Mergers

Place a tile between two companies to trigger a merger! The larger company absorbs the smaller one.

  • 🥇 Majority holder gets the biggest bonus
  • 🥈 Minority holder gets a smaller bonus
  • You can sell, trade, or keep shares of the acquired company

💡 Companies with 11+ tiles are "Safe" and can't be acquired!

5 Winning

The game ends when:

  • Any company reaches 41+ tiles, OR
  • All active companies are Safe (11+ tiles)

All shares are liquidated at current prices. Highest net worth wins!
